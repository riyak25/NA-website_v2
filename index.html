About Us

WHO ARE WE & WHAT WE DO?

Newton's Apple Security Solutions is a cyber security firm providing end to end cyber security solutiosn. We provide a complete suite of vuknerability assesment and penetration testing services as well as security auditing like PCI-DSS, HIPAA, GDPR and ISO 27001

We have an excellent quality and flawless customer experience when it comes to VAPT of IT resources. In the past 2 years, Newton's Apple has tested 100+ applucations and 200+ IT infrastructures ranging from E-commerce, Consumer Internet, Cloud Service Platforms, Fintech, Law, Healthcare and many more.

Our team consists of experienced cyber security experts, bug bounty hunters, security researchers and security analysts. In all, the team is well balanced to handle all your security requirements and provide an effective protection against a range of modern cyber threats.
